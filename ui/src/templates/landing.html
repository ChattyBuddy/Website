Meet our team:

We met in ECE 140B in UC San Diego and decided to revolutionize college learning experience. After taking many many classes, we all agreed that we felt like we were not learning in the most efficient way possible. Therefore, we want to create something that can ensure everyone gets the most out of their classes.
